What you'll learn

Straight from the workbook.
Built for real leaders.

The workshop is built around the official How to Be a Great Boss Workbook — the same tools used by 90,000+ bosses worldwide. Here's what's inside your day.

01
The Four Truths™
It can be simple. Your style doesn't have to change. You must genuinely care about your people — and you must want to be great. The foundation everything else is built on.
02
The Five Leadership Practices™
Give clear direction. Provide the necessary tools. Let go of the vine. Act with the greater good in mind. Take Clarity Breaks®. Five disciplines that define great leadership.
03
Delegate & Elevate®
Stop doing work that belongs to others. Map every activity into Love/Great, Like/Good, Don't Like/Good, and Don't Like/Not Good — then let go of the bottom half.
04
The Five Management Practices™
Keep expectations clear. Communicate well. Have the right meeting pulse. Hold Quarterly Conversations™. Reward and recognize. The daily disciplines that build great teams.
05
The 5-5-5™ & People Analyzer®
The 5-5-5 covers Core Values, Rocks, and Roles — the 90% of what you expect from every direct report. Pair it with the People Analyzer® to see clearly who's in the right seat.
06
Quarterly Conversations™ & The Three-Strike Rule
Hold conversations that build relationships, not dread. And when things go sideways — use the Three-Strike Rule to address performance issues fairly, clearly, and decisively.

FAQ

Questions, answered.

Do we need to be running EOS to attend?
+
Not at all. While the workshop is rooted in the principles of How to Be a Great Boss by Gino Wickman and René Boer, the tools and frameworks apply to any leader in any business. If you manage people, this workshop is for you.
Can I bring my whole leadership team?
+
Absolutely — and we encourage it. Teams who attend together implement faster and hold each other accountable. Contact us about group bookings for your leadership team.
How long is the workshop?
+
The workshop runs a full day from 9:00am to 4:30pm, including breaks and lunch.
Is this available virtually?
+
In-person is strongly preferred for the peer learning components, but a limited number of virtual cohorts run quarterly. Contact us to be added to the virtual waitlist.
What's the cancellation policy?
+
Full refund up to 7 days before the event. Within 7 days, your registration transfers to a future cohort. No-shows are non-refundable but transferable within 90 days.
